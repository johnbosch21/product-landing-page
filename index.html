Location

The Community Pantry is located at 108 Maginhawa Street. As more donations come in and the number of beneficiaries grows, the location only serves now as the drop off center for donations.

Partner Communities

Most of the recipients of donations are residents of barangays in Diliman, Quezon City. We coordinate with leaders/volunteers from these barangays for a systematic and efficient distribution to the benificiaries. Other community pantries across the Philippines also receive aid from MCP.

Volunteers

The Maginhawa Community Pantry is comprised of selfless people from different backgrounds.
